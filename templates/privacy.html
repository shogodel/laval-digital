1. Who We Are

2. What Data We Collect

Account Data: Email address, name, Shopify store domain, billing information processed securely via our payment processor.

Usage Data: Conversation history with AI agents, task execution logs, and campaign preferences. This data is used solely to improve your marketing results.

Essential Cookies: We use only essential session cookies for authentication. No tracking cookies, no analytics cookies, no third-party marketing cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to: (a) operate the AI marketing service, (b) execute marketing tasks you request, (c) improve service quality, and (d) comply with legal obligations. We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Shopify — via OAuth API, solely to execute your marketing tasks

  • Payment processor — for billing (we do not store credit card details)

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon cancellation, your data is deleted within 30 days. Shopify OAuth tokens are revoked immediately upon uninstall.
