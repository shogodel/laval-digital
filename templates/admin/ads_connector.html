🔬 How the Partnership Model Works

1
We own the API access. Laval Digital has a Google Ads Manager Account (MCC) with developer token — we handle all the OAuth and API credentials.
2
Add us as a partner. In your Google Ads account, go to Settings → Account Access and add our MCC ID {{ mcc_id }} as a partner.
3
We discover your accounts. Once you've added us, click "Discover Accounts" above and select which ones to connect.
4
Manage your ads. Our agents can now create campaigns, keywords, ad copy, and reports across your connected accounts.
🔒 No per-user OAuth. You never need to generate API credentials, client IDs, or tokens. Just add our MCC as a partner and you're done.
